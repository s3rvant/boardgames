2x2 Rubik's cube rotates on board from Protoplanets
Randomize so that no side has more than 2 of same color
Players draw matching colored d6 of squares facing them
Everyone gets matching colored cubes of top faces
Players each have 6 cards with listed abilities
Players roll dice with number representing which card they can activate
Cards list 2-3 colors that give special effect for their number
Players assign dice to cards to activate their effects
Players can assign 2 dice to make die either a different number or a different color
Wood cubes are assigned the same with number being wild
Wood cubes can be paired with dice same as 2 dice
The 6 cards are 6 phases that are then resolved in order
